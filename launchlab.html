🔬 Debrief — What Did We Learn?
📊
Data Changes Everything
Compare your Round 1 and Round 2 scores. On average, teams improve significantly when they have data. The cost of NOT doing research is measured in failed launches.
🧠
Intuition Is Biased
Trendy-sounding concepts like NeuroFizz often beat GutPunch on instinct — but consumer data told a different story. Novelty bias and confirmation bias are real strategic risks.
🎯
Alignment Matters
The highest scores came from matching product, price, and channel to the data. A great product at the wrong price in the wrong channel still underperforms. This is marketing strategy.
⚔️
The Herd Penalty
After seeing data, did everyone rush to GutPunch? In real markets, shared insight without differentiation just creates a new red ocean. Strategy means finding where data AND white space overlap.
