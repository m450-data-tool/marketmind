Debrief — What This Simulation Taught Us
🎯
Targeting is Foundation
Teams that found an underserved segment and matched product features to real needs had a structural advantage. STP isn't just a framework — it's the source of competitive position.
💵
Price Signals Value
Pricing above WTP lost volume. Pricing too far below raised quality doubts. The sweet spot reflects what your segment believes the product is worth.
📣
Channel-Segment Fit
A great product at the right price with the wrong media mix still underperforms. Every allocation decision either reinforces or contradicts your positioning.
⚔️
Competitive Dynamics
Teams in the same segment split that market's value. This is why blue ocean strategy delivers outsized returns — and why monitoring competitor moves is a strategic imperative.
